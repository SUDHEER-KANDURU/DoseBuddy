Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a medicine?

Go to Add Medicine in the sidebar. Fill in the medicine name, dosage, instructions, start/end dates, and reminder times. You can also upload a prescription image to auto-fill the form using AI.

How do reminders work?

DoseBuddy sends browser notifications at the exact times you set for each medicine. Make sure to allow notifications when prompted.

How is adherence calculated?

Adherence is the percentage of doses marked as taken out of total scheduled doses for today. Check the Reports page for your 7-day trend.

How does dark mode work?

Toggle dark mode using the Dark Mode button at the bottom of the sidebar. Your preference is saved automatically.

Can a caregiver manage medicines?

Yes. Sign up as a Caretaker and enter the patient email. You can view their schedule but only the patient account can add or delete medicines.
